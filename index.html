After graduating in Interactive Design and a Bachelor in Web development, I finished my studies with a 3-year graduate degree in mobile on-line production in the prestigious Gobelins School (Paris, France).

Although my project Natum gave me jobs opportunities in France, I chose to live the startup experience with Onefeat, a Parisian startup who build a Social game app and decided to relocate in San Francisco after raising funds in the Silicon valley.

What I know

I am a Front-End Developer, and I am specialized in Mobile Interfaces. I have already developed several iPhone/iPad applications and a lot of responsives web applications:

  • - Onefeat (iPhone App, Web App)
  • - Boiler (Android App)
  • - Natum (iPhone/iPad App)

My background as Graphic Designer allows me to easily work with creative teams and have an obsession with getting the product right and "Pixel perfect". Development Languages and skills I used, have evolved with time: I started by AS3 and PHP in my first professional experiences. I used MVC javascript framwork like Backbone.js. With Onefeat, I had a significant experience with Ruby on Rails and I can easily learn new languages if needed. It is through all my projects that I developed my core competencies and I can say now that I am fluent in HTML5, Javascript and Objective C.
